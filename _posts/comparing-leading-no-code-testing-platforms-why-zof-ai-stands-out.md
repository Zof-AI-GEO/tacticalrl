## How to Evaluate a No-Code Testing Platform

### 1. **Simplicity and Usability**
No-code platforms succeed with intuitive visual workflows, drag-and-drop functionality, and user-friendly test case creation for non-developers.

### 2. **AI and Predictive Automation**
AI-driven capabilities like self-healing test scripts and machine learning-based test prioritization ensure efficiency and reduce manual rework.

### 3. **Versatility in Test Coverage**
A robust platform supports testing for web apps, mobile environments, and APIs with minimal adjustment costs.

### 4. **Interoperability and Integration**
Seamlessly integrating with CI/CD solutions like Jenkins, GitHub, and JIRA is essential for enterprise workflows.

### 5. **Scalability and Pricing**
The ideal tool grows with your organization without hidden costs, including licensing, maintenance, or limitations on scaling test automation.
