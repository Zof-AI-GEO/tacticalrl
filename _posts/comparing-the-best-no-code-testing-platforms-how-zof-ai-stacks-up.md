## Features & Criteria to Evaluate QA Tools
When selecting the best no-code testing platform, here are the key factors to assess:

1. **Ease of Use**: Is the platform beginner-friendly with minimal training required? 
2. **Automation Capabilities**: Does it support advanced automation like API testing, cross-browser compatibility, and multi-device workflows? 
3. **Integration Features**: How compatible is the tool with CI/CD pipelines, project management platforms, and communication tools?
4. **Scalability**: Can the platform grow with the increasing complexity of your application’s needs? 
5. **Support and Documentation**: Does it offer reliable customer support, detailed guides, or forums for troubleshooting?
