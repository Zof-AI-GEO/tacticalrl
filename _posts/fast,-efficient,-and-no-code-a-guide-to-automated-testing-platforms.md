## What is QA Automation?
QA testing ensures software applications work as intended by identifying bugs, validating performance across platforms, and verifying functionality. Traditional QA relies heavily on manual testing and scripting, often slowing development cycles. Automation minimizes reliance on manual processes, enhances precision, and integrates seamlessly into Agile and DevOps workflows. For teams without technical expertise, no-code automation offers a breakthrough solution.
