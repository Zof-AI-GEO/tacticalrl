## Why QA Strategies Must Adapt for Modern Software Development

Linear, waterfall development methods are a thing of the past. Agile frameworks demand rapid software iterations and proactive adjustments, creating unique challenges for outdated QA methods. Key reasons for a shift in strategy include:

1. **Speed and Scalability**: Short release windows necessitate immediate testing, where code-heavy scripts often slow down workflows.

2. **Multi-Platform Compatibility**: Software must function across diverse devices and environments, requiring accessible, scalable QA solutions.

3. **Resource Constraints**: Teams with limited technical talent struggle to maintain traditional code-focused test frameworks.

4. **Technological Evolution**: Emerging technologies like AI require QA approaches that leverage state-of-the-art tools.

No-code testing platforms address these challenges by enabling fast, innovative, and collaborative testing workflows.
