No-code testing democratizes software development, enabling non-technical personnel to actively contribute to QA processes. Software teams in 2025 are leveraging these platforms to:

1. **Boost Time-to-Market**: Replace scripted testing with drag-and-drop functionality for faster iterations.
2. **Foster Collaboration**: Unite business and IT teams for seamless workflows.
3. **Cut Costs**: Reduce dependency on specialized testers.
