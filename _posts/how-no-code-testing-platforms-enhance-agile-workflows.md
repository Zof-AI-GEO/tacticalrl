## What Are Agile Workflows in QA?

Agile workflows focus on delivering smaller, functional product increments in short cycles—sprints. These workflows depend on continuous integration (CI), continuous delivery (CD), and cross-team collaboration. QA is embedded throughout the sprint, requiring iterative testing before, during, and post-coding to ensure top-quality releases. Challenges like repetitive testing, human error, and aligning QA with development cycles demand a solution—no-code testing.
