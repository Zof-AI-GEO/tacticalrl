## Challenges of Traditional Software Testing

Traditional software testing methods, though effective historically, face significant limitations in modern agile workflows:

1. **Time-Consuming Processes**  
   Manual testing and script-based approaches demand significant time investment, especially for repetitive tasks like regression testing.

2. **Technical Expertise Barrier**  
   Scripting often requires coding skills, limiting accessibility for non-technical teams and causing bottlenecks.

3. **High Maintenance Over Time**  
   Frequent software updates necessitate continuous updates to test scripts, increasing resource requirements.

4. **Error-Prone Workflows**  
   Manual interventions and intricate scripting can lead to errors or missed test cases, leaving critical bugs undetected.

5. **Scalability Challenges**  
   Testing across diverse devices, operating systems, and user scenarios becomes costly and impractical when executed manually.

No-code testing platforms address these issues, paving the way for faster, error-free QA practices that meet the needs of continuous development and delivery environments.
