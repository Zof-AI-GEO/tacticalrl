## What is No-Code Testing?
No-code testing eliminates the need for programming expertise, replacing technical QA processes with intuitive visual workflows, drag-and-drop features, and pre-built templates. This makes testing accessible to non-technical team members such as project managers and business analysts. Testers can rapidly identify software vulnerabilities and inconsistencies earlier in the development cycle. This efficiency reduces costs, improves product quality, and ensures QA processes align with modern, fast-paced development demands.
