## Why Streamlined QA Matters in Today's Agile Development

Agile and DevOps methodologies demand rapid, ongoing development with minimal errors. The challenge lies in keeping up with short timelines while ensuring software reliability. Traditional testing—with its scripting complexities and time-intensive manual processes—struggles to meet these demands efficiently.

No-code testing tools fill this gap by democratizing the QA process. Developers and testers alike can now create automated test scenarios without writing code. QA becomes less about technical expertise and more about collaboration, empowering teams to ship faster and more effectively.
