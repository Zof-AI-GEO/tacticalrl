## Core Factors to Evaluate When Choosing No-Code Testing Tools

### 1. **Assess Your Team’s Needs and Workflow**
Understanding your team’s technical expertise and existing processes is crucial. Does the platform cater to both beginners and experienced testers? It’s essential to select a tool that balances intuitiveness with robust functionality.

### 2. **Ease of Use and Time to Deploy**
Usability directly impacts team adoption. Opt for platforms offering streamlined interfaces and minimal setup requirements—like Zof AI, which simplifies workflows with drag-and-drop functionality and pre-built templates.

### 3. **System Compatibility**
Ensure the platform supports your team’s development environment, browsers, and devices. Misaligned compatibility can incur unexpected costs.

### 4. **Affordability and Cost Efficiency**
Evaluate subscription plans and long-term ownership costs. Compare monthly versus annual plans as well as scalability options.

### 5. **Automation Features**
Automation is critical for optimizing testing processes. Look for tools offering features like:
  - **Automated Test Creation**: Generate cases with minimal manual input.
  - **Continuous Testing**: Schedule recurring tests to validate features automatically.
  - **Bug Alerts**: Reduce issue turnarounds with instant notifications.
