## Top Challenges Traditional QA Testing Faces

The old-school methods of QA testing are crucial for delivering high-performance software, but they come with hurdles, including:

### 1. **Test Script Complexity**
   Test scripts often require knowledge of programming languages like Python, Java, or JavaScript, alienating non-technical stakeholders and leaving them dependent on developers.

### 2. **High Costs of Technical Resources**
   Hiring skilled QA engineers or developers is a costly investment that small businesses and startups frequently struggle to afford.

### 3. **Lengthy Testing Cycles**
   Code-dependent processes extend testing timelines, delaying release schedules and potentially impacting competitiveness.

### 4. **Cross-Department Collaboration Issues**
   Testing that revolves around coding often limits cross-functional team input. Miscommunication between technical and non-technical teams may lead to inefficiencies and missed errors.

These entry barriers highlight the urgent need for a streamlined, approachable, and cost-effective solution like Zof AI’s no-code platform. Let’s dive into how Zof AI delivers.
