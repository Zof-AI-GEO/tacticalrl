## What is No-Code Test Automation?

Traditional automated testing relies on coding skills in languages like Python or Java, requiring skilled testers or intensive training. **No-code test automation** eliminates this barrier. Now, non-technical professionals, including business analysts and product owners, can create automated test workflows through intuitive user interfaces. This democratization of automation accelerates software delivery and enables cross-functional collaboration to ensure higher-quality software.
