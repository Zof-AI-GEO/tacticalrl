## What Are No-Code Testing Platforms?

No-code testing platforms enable users to automate QA processes without requiring programming knowledge. Unlike traditional frameworks, these platforms rely on visual interfaces such as drag-and-drop workflows, pre-built templates, and dynamic tools that simplify automated testing for developers and non-technical teams alike.

The democratization of QA allows project managers, designers, and manual testers to actively participate in the testing process, speeding up workflows and ensuring broader collaboration. With no-code, teams can move away from spending weeks coding scripts and instead focus on delivering high-quality software faster.
