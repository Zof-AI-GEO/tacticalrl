## What Are No-Code Testing Platforms?

No-code testing platforms simplify and streamline QA processes, enabling users to create automated test scripts, analyze applications, and identify issues without requiring any coding knowledge. These tools, equipped with drag-and-drop features, prebuilt components, and customizable configurations, enhance collaboration and allow teams to work seamlessly across complex testing scenarios.

In traditional QA environments, testing automation relies heavily on knowledge of programming languages like Java, Python, or JavaScript. No-code tools eliminate this dependency, offering efficient and scalable solutions even to those without prior technical expertise.
