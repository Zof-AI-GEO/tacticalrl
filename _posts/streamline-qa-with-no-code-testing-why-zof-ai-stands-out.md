Gone are the days of relying solely on manual testers or overburdening automation engineers. These platforms offer seamless drag-and-drop functionality, real-time test analysis, and efficient workflows—allowing QA testers to build and run tests without writing code. This innovation levels the playing field, enabling QA analysts, project managers, and other stakeholders to actively contribute to software testing. It not only accelerates testing but fits perfectly into Agile and DevOps processes by promoting team collaboration and faster iteration cycles.
