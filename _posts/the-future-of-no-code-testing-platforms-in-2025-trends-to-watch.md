## What Are No-Code Testing Platforms?

No-code testing platforms allow users to create, execute, and manage test cases without needing programming expertise. They democratize testing processes, empowering teams to focus more on strategy and innovation while relying on automation for repetitive tasks.

Using intuitive visual workflows, seamless integration capabilities, and automated processes, no-code tools enhance testing efficiency across areas like UI testing, performance testing, and regression testing. By minimizing human errors and accelerating iterations, these platforms play a pivotal role in modern software development lifecycles.
