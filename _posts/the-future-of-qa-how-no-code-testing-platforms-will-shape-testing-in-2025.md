## Introduction to No-Code Testing Platforms

No-code testing platforms enable QA teams to develop, run, and manage tests without relying on traditional coding. Users build tests via intuitive drag-and-drop interfaces and AI-powered features, eliminating the need for programming skills.

### Remarkable Features of No-Code Platforms:
- **Drag-and-drop test case creation** for ease and simplicity.
- **Automated testing** without extensive technical knowledge.
- **Seamless integrations** with CI/CD pipelines.
- **AI-driven adaptability** to maintain tests autonomously during software changes.

These tools democratize testing for non-technical professionals, enhance collaboration across teams, and reduce cost and time barriers.
