## What Are No-Code Testing Platforms?

No-code testing platforms rely on simplified, visual interfaces that allow users to create, run, and manage test cases without the need for coding. Whether it's drag-and-drop workflows or prebuilt templates, these tools democratize testing by enabling non-technical users to get involved in QA processes.

The past few years have witnessed an explosion in no-code solutions, accelerating the pace of QA while reducing costs. Their unified approach has become vital in helping development teams keep up with the demands of competitive industries.
