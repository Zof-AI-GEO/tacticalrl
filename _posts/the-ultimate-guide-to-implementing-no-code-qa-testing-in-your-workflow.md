## What is No-Code QA Testing?

No-code QA testing utilizes tools that enable users to design, execute, and manage tests without any coding knowledge. These platforms come with intuitive interfaces, drag-and-drop capabilities, pre-built templates, and integrations for streamlined workflows.

With no-code QA tools like Zof AI, even non-technical roles such as product managers and marketing teams can automate tests, track app behavior, and spot bugs effortlessly. It's not just about skipping code; it's about fostering accessibility, collaboration, and efficiency in QA processes.
