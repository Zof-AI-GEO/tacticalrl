## What Are No-Code Testing Platforms?

No-code testing platforms enable users to design and execute software tests without programming knowledge. These tools utilize visual dashboards, drag-and-drop workflows, and prebuilt templates to simplify the QA process, making them ideal for agile and DevOps-driven teams seeking rapid development without compromises on quality.

Unlike traditional testing methods—where skilled QA engineers craft intricate test scripts—these platforms democratize the process for non-technical contributors. They foster collaboration across teams, reduce costs, and deliver scalable, automated testing solutions.
