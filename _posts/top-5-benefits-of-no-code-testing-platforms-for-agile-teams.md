## What Are No-Code Testing Platforms?

No-code testing platforms empower users to design, execute, and manage automated software tests without coding. Featuring intuitive visual editors and user-friendly interfaces, these platforms allow QA engineers, project managers, and non-developers to actively contribute to testing activities.

For agile teams tackling rapid development cycles and tight deadlines, no-code testing offers significant advantages over conventional code-heavy testing approaches. By democratizing the testing process, these platforms reduce bottlenecks in workflows and ensure seamless collaboration.
