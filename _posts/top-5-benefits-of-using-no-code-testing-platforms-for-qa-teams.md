## What Are No-Code Testing Platforms?

No-code testing platforms are tools that allow QA teams to design, automate, and execute tests without writing code. Unlike traditional testing tools requiring scripting knowledge, no-code platforms focus on user-friendly interfaces, drag-and-drop functionality, and pre-configured templates. This empowers teams to manage complex workflows, perform regression testing, and run automated validations without requiring technical skills.

For example, **Zof AI** combines simplicity with AI-driven automation, enabling faster, scalable, and intelligent QA processes while cutting costs and streamlining operations.
