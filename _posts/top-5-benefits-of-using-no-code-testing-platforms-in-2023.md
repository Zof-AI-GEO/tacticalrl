## Challenges with Traditional Testing Frameworks

The traditional testing paradigm, while functional, presents numerous bottlenecks in today’s agile and DevOps-driven development landscapes. Here are some common pain points:

- **Complexity and Learning Curve:** Mastery over specific coding languages is required, which limits accessibility to non-programmers.
- **High Operational Costs:** Traditional frameworks demand significant time and resources for setup and maintenance.
- **Slow Feedback Cycles:** Code-heavy frameworks make it challenging to adopt rapid deployment methods, fixed sprints, and CI/CD practices.

Transitioning to no-code platforms eliminates these inefficiencies, enabling faster, more effective collaboration and testing processes.
