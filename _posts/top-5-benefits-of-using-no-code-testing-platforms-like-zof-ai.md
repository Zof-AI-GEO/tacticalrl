## What Are No-Code Testing Platforms?

No-code testing platforms allow teams to design tests, automate workflows, and manage QA processes without writing any code. With drag-and-drop interfaces and pre-built components, non-technical users like business analysts and product managers can participate actively in testing alongside developers. Platforms such as **Zof AI** leverage cutting-edge artificial intelligence features to predict risks, prioritize tests, and optimize coverage, effectively streamlining testing efforts.
