## The Role of No-Code Testing in Accelerating Software Development

Modern software cycles demand speed and collaboration. No-code platforms remove programming dependencies, helping teams focus on iterative development. They allow **non-technical users**, like product managers or designers, to join the testing process effortlessly.

### Key Benefits of No-Code Testing
1. Accessible to all users, regardless of coding skills. 
2. Accelerates testing compatible with Agile/DevOps workflows.
3. Cost-efficient and reduces dependency on QA engineers. 
4. Promotes team collaboration by democratizing testing. 
5. Scalable for growing businesses.
