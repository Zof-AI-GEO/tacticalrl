## Why Choose No-Code Testing in 2023?

### 1. **Enhanced Agility for Frequent Updates**
For businesses embracing Agile or DevOps workflows, traditional testing lags behind. No-code tools expedite test case creation, execution, and updates, ensuring on-time releases.

### 2. **User-Friendly for Non-Technical Teams**
No-code platforms allow non-technical team members to actively contribute, streamlining collaboration while reducing dependencies on seasoned developers.

### 3. **Cost-Efficient and Time-Saving**
By automating repetitive tests, these platforms reduce not only time but also eliminate costs associated with expert coding.

### 4. **Higher Accuracy Rates**
With AI-led automated tests, human errors are minimized, enhancing the reliability of your QA outcomes.
