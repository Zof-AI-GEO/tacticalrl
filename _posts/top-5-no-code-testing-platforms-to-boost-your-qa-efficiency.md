## What Makes a Great No-Code Testing Platform?

Not all no-code testing platforms are equal—some excel in specific features, while others prioritize usability. Here are the essential criteria for selecting a no-code testing tool:

1. **Ease of Use:** Intuitive interface empowering non-technical users to create and run tests effortlessly.
2. **Integrations:** Seamless integration with CI/CD workflows, project management tools, and reporting systems.
3. **Flexibility:** Configurations, reusable templates, or in-built logic creators for complex scenarios.
4. **Reporting:** Actionable insights for rapid issue resolution.
5. **Scalability:** Effective performance for both small teams and large organizations handling significant test volumes.
