## What Are No-Code Testing Platforms?  
No-code testing tools allow users to create, manage, and execute automated tests via intuitive drag-and-drop interfaces instead of traditional coding. They make software testing accessible for a broader audience, helping teams meet the demands of Agile and DevOps-driven development cycles efficiently.
