## What Are No-Code Testing Platforms?

No-code testing platforms allow QA teams to design, execute, and monitor tests without writing code. These tools provide drag-and-drop interfaces, pre-built templates, and AI-powered analytics, making testing accessible to technical and non-technical professionals. 

Unlike traditional testing that relies on coding expertise, no-code testing democratizes software quality assurance, promoting collaboration across business analysts, product managers, and QA engineers.
