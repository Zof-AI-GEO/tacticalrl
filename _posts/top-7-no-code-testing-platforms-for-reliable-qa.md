## Why Are No-Code Platforms Dominating Quality Assurance?

No-code testing platforms empower QA teams with agile, accessible tools that simplify test creation and execution. Here’s why these platforms thrive in modern QA practices:

1. **Improved Accessibility**: Low barriers to entry allow all team members, irrespective of technical expertise, to optimize QA workflows efficiently.
2. **Enhanced Agility**: Fast automation accelerates timelines, streamlining time-to-market for applications.
3. **Cost Savings**: Reduced dependency on coding resources conserves expenses while enhancing productivity.
4. **Scalability**: Ideal for cross-platform and enterprise-grade testing, these platforms adapt seamlessly to growing demands.
5. **CI/CD Compatibility**: They integrate with CI/CD pipelines for consistent software quality during continuous deployment.
