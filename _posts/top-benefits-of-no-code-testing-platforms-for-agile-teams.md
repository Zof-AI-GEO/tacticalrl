## Challenges Agile Teams Face in Traditional QA Processes

Traditional QA processes often hinder Agile teams used to flexibility and speed. Here are some of the most common challenges:

1. **Manual Testing Bottlenecks**  
Manual testing is time-consuming and error-prone, creating delays that clash with Agile's frequent iteration cycles.

2. **Ineffective Collaboration**  
Siloed QA practices diminish cross-functional teamwork, a core tenet of Agile methodologies.

3. **Slow Feedback Loops**  
Rigid QA processes fail to align with quick Agile sprints, leaving teams struggling to maintain responsiveness.

4. **Regression Testing Challenges**  
Frequent updates complicate regression testing, which traditional workflows cannot scale effectively.

5. **Limited Automation Potential**  
Outdated testing tools demand scripting know-how, slowing test creation and hindering scalability.

These roadblocks lead to workflow inefficiencies and missed opportunities for Agile teams to maximize their speed and impact. This is where no-code platforms step in.
