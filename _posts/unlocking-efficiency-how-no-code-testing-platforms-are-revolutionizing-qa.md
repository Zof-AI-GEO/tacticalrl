## The Challenges of Traditional Testing

Traditional QA workflows face key hurdles:

1. **Technical Skill Dependency**: Only skilled coders can effectively write test scripts.
2. **Time Constraints**: Script creation and maintenance are time-consuming.
3. **High Costs**: Overheads from hiring, training, and managing testing teams escalate expenses.
4. **Scalability Issues**: Expanding traditional testing operations for large projects creates inefficiencies.

These challenges highlight the need for innovative, scalable solutions—paving the way for no-code platforms.
