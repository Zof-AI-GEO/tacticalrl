## The Evolution of Software Testing Methodologies

QA has undergone a drastic evolution, from manual testing to automated testing tools such as Selenium. While automated testing improved efficiency, it required programming expertise and significant maintenance. Enter no-code testing—bringing intuitive, accessible solutions to QA teams. No-code platforms enable users to create, execute, and manage automated tests without coding knowledge, making QA processes faster and collaborative.
